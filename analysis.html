REASONABLE HYPOTHESIS

  • Rapid weight gain Nov 2025→Jan 2026 (+4.4 kg in ~6 weeks) is likely related to the documented pregnancy
  • Movement decline precedes full functional collapse by 4–8 weeks (Jan→Apr 2026 trajectory)
  • Food restriction represents control-seeking in response to emotional dysregulation, not only appetite loss
  • The Yosef push-pull cycle is unstable partly because the relationship simultaneously serves as regulatory resource and source of dysregulation
  • Weight decoupling in 2026 is partly attributable to Paroxetine's weight-gain profile
  • Academic structure functions as a protective behavioral scaffold
  • 3.9h sleep Jul–Sep 2024 was an early signal, not a consequence, of the Episode A arc

SPECULATION

  • A seasonal pattern exists — insufficient data (opposite seasons across two episodes)
  • Menstrual cycle timing influences mood stability — no cross-reference data
  • The 2022 weight loss trajectory was the first manifestation of a depressive process that became symptomatic 2+ years later
  • Mindfulness session frequency changes before episodes
  • Substance use is part of a broader disinhibition response — only one documented instance

Medication adherence unknown
Only start dates documented. Dose, consistency of use, and response monitoring absent.
Sleep data insufficient
64 nights from one 3-month window. Quality, stages, and longitudinal patterns cannot be assessed.
Medication effects confounded
Paroxetine introduction (Jan 29) and assault (March) are too close to separate. Efficacy cannot be assessed.
Causality vs. correlation
All temporal associations are correlational. Directionality (does low activity cause low mood, or vice versa?) cannot be determined.
Journal selection bias
Entries cluster at crisis points. Stable periods are under-documented, inflating apparent association between entries and crisis states.
Diagnostic status unknown
This analysis describes patterns only. "High then crash," "relational reactivity," and "restriction as control" fit several different clinical pictures with different treatment implications.
